Why work in product marketing?

The average customer’s expectations have never been higher. Product marketers are key to tackling these challenges. The skill set is in high demand.
Many people “fell” into product marketing. Product marketers come from different backgrounds. It’s a flexible career to pivot into.
Product marketing is a great opportunity to think outside of the box, be challenged to come up with new ideas, and leverage storytelling.
Product marketing is cross-functional and strategic. Product marketers are great candidates for entrepreneurship and executive leadership.
